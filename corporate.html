Corporate Governance

Following the core of balancing the company

Corporate Governance Guidelines of PT. Bima Mineral Indonesia is the basis for implementing the principles of Transparency, Accountability, Responsibility, Independence and Fairness to improve the company's performance and image.

The Guidelines for Corporate Governance are a reference for implementing Good Corporate Governance in making decisions, carrying out actions based on high morals, complying with laws and regulations and awareness of corporate social responsibility for interested parties (stakeholders).

The processes and structures used by the Company in managing the Company are based on the principles of Good Corporate Governance

Transparency

Openness in carrying out the decision-making process and openness in presenting material and relevant information regarding Company

Independence

A situation where the Company is managed professionally without conflict of interest and influence or pressure from any party that is not in accordance with the applicable laws and principles healthy corporation

Accountability

Clarity of functions, implementation and accountability Company so that the management of the company is carried out effectively

Responsibility

The suitability in the management of the Company towards prevailing laws and regulations and corporate principles healthy

Fairness

Fairness and equality in fulfilling stakeholder rights interests arising based on agreements and applicable laws and regulations
